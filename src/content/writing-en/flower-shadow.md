---
title: "Flowers Toying with Shadows"
date: 2019-11-21
description: "A short poem: fireworks, passing geese, passing wind."
tags: ["poem"]
---

In the distance, silent fireworks bloom across half the night sky,  
then hurry off the stage.  
The belated blast bursts my eardrums.  
I stare wider — the void is pitch black.

Geese pass and leave a trace;  
what cannot be held is what slips through the fingers.  
Wind passes and leaves a sound;  
what cannot be stopped is the echo by the ear.
